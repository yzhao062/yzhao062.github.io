\section{\sc Research Summary}
I build the methods, benchmarks, and open-source tools for \textbf{AI auditing}---making
AI systems inspectable, safe, and accountable. This spans how to inspect and evaluate
AI systems (auditing and assurance), what failure modes to audit for (safety and security),
and where accountability is not optional (science and society).
